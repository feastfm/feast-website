About

FEAST is a festival of art and sustainable technology. It is about local face-to-face engagement while at the same time creating new forms of global conversation.

Our Team

FEAST is an arts-led organisation. We are structured as a co-op in which members are encouraged to show there work, and propose new projects to work on with other members. there are many ways to get involved, from a creative perspective, or a production perspective. Whether it is research, writing, or hands on people skills. Our current team is growing slowly and organically, To join send an email to join@feat.fm.
